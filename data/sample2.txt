Company Leave Policy
Our leave policy is designed to support your well-being and ensure a healthy work-life balance. We believe that taking time off to recharge is essential for long-term success and productivity.

1. Annual Leave
Entitlement: Employees are entitled to 12 business days of paid annual leave per year.
Eligibility: Leave credits are available after completing 12 consecutive months of service.
Request Process: Please submit leave requests through the HR portal at least 7 business days in advance (except for emergencies).

2. Sick Leave
Employees who are unable to work due to illness must notify their direct supervisor or HR before the start of the workday.
Medical Certificate: A valid doctor's note is required for any sick leave lasting more than 2 consecutive business days.

3. Compassionate & Special Leave
The company provides paid time off for significant life events, including:
Employee Marriage: 3 business days.
Death of an Immediate Family Member: 2 business days.
Paternity Leave: 2 business days (for fathers).

4. Maternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 3 months of paid maternity leave (typically 1.5 months before and 1.5 months after delivery) in accordance with local labor laws.

5. Public Holidays
The company observes all official national public holidays.
Guidelines regarding "Collective Leave" (Cuti Bersama) will be issued via internal memo at the beginning of each calendar year.

6. Leave Carry-Over & Expiration
Unused annual leave expires at the end of the calendar year and cannot be cashed out, unless a specific exception is granted by management.
We strongly encourage all employees to utilize their full leave entitlement to rest and stay healthy.

Note: All leave requests require approval from the respective Department Manager to ensure consistent team coverage.
